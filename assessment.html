The project will be assessed on

  • the quality of the project deliverable(s), and
  • the overall management of the development process.

Calculation of Final Mark

The final mark for each team member will be composed of:

  • a mark for the group project (90%), subdivided into:
    • progress presentation (20%)
    • final group report (60%)
    • final presentation (10%)
  • a mark for the individual report (10%)

The marks for the group project will be distributed amongst the team members in proportion to each member's overall contribution to the project.

A copy of the standards for allocating marks is also available.
